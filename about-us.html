Our Story


Founded in 2024, RJ Careers started with the goal of offering a better way for students and professionals in Rajasthan to find opportunities tailored to their location and ambitions. Over time, we expanded our services to include all industries in Rajasthan, creating a bridge between job seekers and employers across the state. Today, our platform serves thousands of users, and we continue to evolve, keeping pace with the changing job market to offer the best possible experience.
